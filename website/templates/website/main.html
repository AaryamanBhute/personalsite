FTC Robotics - Knightrix

Programming Lead

Chantilly, Virginia

Sept 2019 - March 2021

As the programming lead I collaborated within the programming division and with the mechanical division to identify objectives and assign the objective to the appropriate team member. I personally contributed to the computer vision program built to identify the "GAME BLOCKS". These were targets placed on the same field as our robot. With this created program, we were able to accurately identify a "GAME BLOCK" and use it to fulfill the game objectives.

Additionally, I worked to revamp the drive and intake mechanism to incorporate encoders to find the position of the robot on the field and the position of the intake mechanism in relation to the robot. My experience on the robotics team gave me a lot of experience programming in a team environment and making updates in a high-stress situation such as in the few minutes between competetive games.
